Our Story

Built For
Every Body

Since 1999, Platinum Athletic Club has been the heartbeat of Surrey's fitness community. Family-owned and operated, we built this club around one conviction — that world-class training belongs to everyone, regardless of experience level or background.

Our 34,000 sq ft facility is more than a gym. It's where Surrey families, competitive athletes, and first-timers come together with the support of expert coaches and multilingual staff fluent in English, Punjabi, and Hindi.

Cancellations must be done in person at the club with written notice, per your membership agreement. We recommend keeping a copy of your cancellation form for your records. If you have any billing concerns after cancellation, please contact us directly at (604) 501-9601 and we'll resolve them promptly.

We offer both contract and month-to-month membership options. Annual memberships offer the best monthly rate with a 12-month commitment. Month-to-month plans are available with more flexibility. Ask our staff about the current options and any ongoing promotions when you visit or call.

Absolutely. We welcome everyone to come in for a free tour of the facility with no obligation. You'll get to see all 34,000 sq ft, meet our staff, and ask any questions you have before making any commitment. Just call ahead or walk in during our operating hours.

Yes. Our 5,000 sq ft ladies-only section is a permanent part of the facility — not a time-restricted zone. It features its own cardio and strength equipment designed for privacy and comfort, available during all regular operating hours.

Yes, we have on-site childminding services so parents can train without worry. Availability and any associated fees depend on your membership type. Ask our front desk for current childminding hours and policies.
