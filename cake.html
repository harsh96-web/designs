A Family Legacy

Where East Meets West in Every Bite

It began in a small kitchen in the early 1970s. Anna Cheung, newly arrived in Canada, started baking the way she always had — with precision, patience, and love. Combining the delicate, airy techniques of European pastry with the bold, natural flavours of Asian baking, she created something Vancouver had never quite tasted before.

In 1974, Anna, Ronald, and Kathleen Cheung opened the first location. Over fifty years later, the Cheung family still runs every bakery — and every product is still made from scratch, every single day, with no artificial colours or flavours. Ever.

"We make every product as if we were going to serve it ourselves — or someone in our own family."
— Anna Cheung, Founder
