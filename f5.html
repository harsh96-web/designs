Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your visit. Still have questions? Just call or message us.

Book a Consultation
How long does keratin smoothening last?+
Our Brazilian keratin treatment typically lasts 3–5 months depending on your hair type, how often you wash it, and the products you use at home. We recommend sulfate-free shampoos to extend the life of the treatment.
Do you offer bridal trial sessions?+
Absolutely — and we strongly recommend it. A bridal trial is a full run of your wedding look done 3–6 weeks before your big day. It gives you time to refine the look and arrive on your wedding day with complete confidence.
Can you come to my venue on the wedding day?+
Yes! Our on-location service brings the full salon experience to your hotel suite, venue, or home. We charge a travel fee starting from $80 CAD depending on distance. Early morning slots are available for wedding day bookings.
What should I bring to my first appointment?+
Bring 2–3 inspiration photos you love, and arrive with clean, dry hair unless instructed otherwise. For makeup appointments, come with a clean moisturized face. If it's a color consultation, avoid washing your hair 24 hours prior.
How far in advance should I book bridal services?+
We recommend booking bridal packages 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak season (May–October). For just hair or makeup without a full package, 4–8 weeks notice is usually sufficient.
Do you have a cancellation policy?+
We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a 50% service fee. For bridal packages, a non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date.
