The Girl Behind the Braids

Seven years of braids, curls, and beautiful brides.

I trained formally in India, spent four years in a full-service salon outside Delhi where I learned the discipline — 25-client weeks, every bride's family watching over my shoulder, every strand counted — and relocated to Canada in 2023.

I'm happiest in the finish. A bridal look can be complicated on paper and still feel off if the braid isn't balanced, the curls aren't sealed, the flowers aren't placed with intention. The difference between a good bridal style and one a bride keeps talking about is almost always the last ten minutes.

I specialize in South Asian bridal — waterfall and fishtail braids, rose-bun architecture, floral placement with gypsophila and fresh blooms — and the dimensional curl work that carries a look from the pheras to the reception. Western bridal, balayage, precision cutting, and smoothing treatments are home too. Every girl deserves to leave the chair feeling more like herself.

— Gurbina
